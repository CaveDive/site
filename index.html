About

"Cave Dive" is a casual timekiller game where you play as a spelunker bot, exploring an infinite cave system. Collect resources, complete assignments, unlock new pickaxes and sharpen your skills traveling deeper and deeper into the caverns, uncovering increasingly challenging environments and hazards.
